About Okilo

Who Are We?

Okilo Integrated Hub Ltd (OIH) is a Lagos-based technology and product company focused on transforming ideas into impactful, user-centered solutions. Founded by Okezie Chukukere, OIH operates at the intersection of culture, technology, and community.

We are not a typical digital agency. As a product-focused company, our purpose is to use technology to foster meaningful human connections. From learning platforms to narrative-driven games, every venture begins with the user and is designed for measurable impact.

Our Mission

We turn ideas into user-focused technology that preserves culture, amplifies underrepresented narratives, and shapes a creative, purposeful future. Our products connect communities, restore heritage, and empower users globally.

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Okezie Chukukere is an entrepreneur, strategist, and ecosystem builder with a singular mission: to make Africa the world's next great innovation frontier. With roots in Lagos and a track record spanning multiple industries, he launched Okilo Integrated Hub to give Africa's next generation the tools, capital, and connections they need to win on a global stage.

His philosophy is simple — when you empower one entrepreneur and one story, you transform an entire community. Every product built at OIH is a direct expression of that belief.

Narrative Gaming

Danfo Hustle

Danfo Hustle is OIH's gaming label and one of its two flagship product lines. It offers story-driven, culturally grounded mobile games for a global audience, beginning with the streets of Lagos.

Lasgidi Payback is the first chapter — an arcade driving and narrative adventure game set in Lagos. Players navigate real routes, build transport empires, meet authentic characters, and complete community missions.

  • Arcade driving & narrative adventure in Lasgidi
  • Real Lagos routes & authentic characters
  • Empire-building mechanics & fleet management
  • Community missions & cultural events
