About Okilo

Who Are We?

Okilo Integrated Hub Ltd (OIH) is a technology and product company based in Lagos. We turn ideas into practical, user-focused solutions. Founded by Okezie Chukukere, OIH brings together culture, technology, and community across all our work.

We are not a typical digital agency. Our primary goal is to use technology to help people connect meaningfully. Whether we are building learning platforms or story-based games, we always start with the user and strive to make a real difference.

Our Mission

We develop user-focused technology that preserves culture, amplifies underrepresented narratives, and shapes a creative, purposeful future. Our products connect communities, restore heritage, and empower users worldwide.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized product house rooted in cultural identity, known for developing platforms that make African narratives accessible, celebrated, and preserved for future generations.

Core Values

What Drives Us

Cultural Rootedness

Our products are grounded in cultural context. Culture is not an added feature — it is the foundation of our work.

Purpose-Driven Innovation

We create experiences that entertain, educate, and foster empathy while authentically representing underrepresented cultures.

Community Engagement

Integrity

Diaspora & Homeland Unity

Flagship Product

Danfo Hustle

Danfo Hustle is OIH's gaming label and flagship product line. It offers story-driven, culturally grounded mobile games for a global audience, beginning with the streets of Lagos.

Lasgidi Payback is the first chapter — an arcade driving and narrative adventure game set in Lagos. Players navigate real routes, build transport empires, meet authentic characters, and complete community missions.

  • Arcade driving & narrative adventure in Lasgidi
  • Real Lagos routes & authentic characters
  • Empire-building mechanics & fleet management
  • Community missions & cultural events
