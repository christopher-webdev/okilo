Who We Are

Founded in Nigeria, Okilo Integrated Hub is a forward-thinking technology company committed to creating cutting-edge digital products and solutions. Our skilled team is passionate about developing engaging mobile games, building advanced e-commerce platforms, and designing custom digital products.

Within this ecosystem, one of our flagship products is Danfo Hustle. At its core, it is a game; bold, playful, and rooted in the everyday rhythm of urban life. Yet it is also more than a game: a gift of inspiration that captures culture, tells stories, and builds community. Danfo Hustle embodies our belief that technology can be entertaining while still carrying deeper meaning and impact.

Our Flagship Experience

Danfo Hustle

Step into the heartbeat of Lagos with Danfo Hustle — a mobile game that captures the spirit, chaos, and rhythm of Nigeria’s bustling streets.

More than just entertainment, Danfo Hustle is a cultural experience. Drive iconic yellow buses, navigate real-life city routes, and meet characters inspired by everyday hustlers. Every ride tells a story — one of resilience, energy, and community.

With rich storytelling, immersive visuals, and authentic vibes, Danfo Hustle connects players to the essence of urban Africa — turning every level into a journey through the soul of the city.
